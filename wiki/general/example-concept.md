# Example Concept

> 🌱 **Seed page** — demonstrates the concept format for `wiki/general/`. Delete once real
> content arrives. Sibling page types: sources, entities.

A concept page accumulates everything the wiki knows about one idea, drawn from across many
sources.

## Definition
A clear, one-paragraph explanation.

## What we know
- Point, with citation `(see [[raw/source.md]])`. `[C:high]`
- Related point, linking [[another-concept]]. `[C:med]`

## Relationships
- How this connects to neighbouring ideas and entities.

## Open questions
- Gaps in what we know about this concept — mirror the important ones into `questions.md`.
